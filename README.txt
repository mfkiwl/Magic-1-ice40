These boards are designed to replicate Bill Buzbee's Magic-1 computer at 

http://www.homebrewcpu.com

They are licenced as Open Source Hardware

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Open-source_hardware

Notes:

Cards are 160x233 euro card with 2 DIN 41612 C96 connectors

They are designed to be used in a Schroff EuropacPRO enclosure

The holes on the outside edge of the card (opposite to the connectors)
are designed to accept the Schroff card extractors ref 24560-378
You will also need to add ref 24560-377 for each card slot for this to work
